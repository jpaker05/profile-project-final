Hi, I am Jessica Paker

Front-end Developer, based in the USA.

My previous studies and degrees are in Archeology and Art History (undergrad), and Art Business (graduate). Art Buisness led me to an interest in the tech field along with a desire to be more mobile. I began my coding journey in August 2022 when I enrolled in a multi-certification coding bootcamp called SheCodes. Starting from the basics, I learned the foundations of HTML and CSS. Then I began learning JavaScript and further developing HTML and CSS techniques. After, I dove into loops and arrays, Bootstrap, Git/GitHub, and hosting on platforms such as Netlify.

I hit a wall while coding my live weather application using APIs for the first time. Instead of calling it quits, I was determined to continue my coding journey. After a week of troubleshooting, searching Stack Overflow, and reaching out on Slack, I decided the best course of action was to destroy and re-build the entire application line-by-line. In the end, my application worked successfully but more importantly, I realized where my mistakes had been, how to make my code cleaner and more effective, and finally it was at this point, I knew my future lay in coding. For my next application using API's, I was then able to quickly build a live world clock application. From here I moved into learning how to make applications responsive across multiple platforms as well as being SEO friendly. My latest certification is in React.

I look forward to working in an environment that is challenging and encouraging in furthering my coding development.
